Welcome to the Test Selection Tool

This is the ISTA test selection tool. This web based system allows ISTA members and non-members to determine an appropriate test protocol(s) for testing their packaged-product and serves as a conduit for contacting ISTA certified testing laboratories who can perform the testing.
